Chicken Biryani

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken
  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 2 onions (fried)
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 tbsp biryani masala

Steps

  1. Cook chicken with spices.
  2. Boil rice 70%.
  3. Layer chicken and rice, steam 20 min.

Chicken Karahi

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken
  • 3 tomatoes
  • 2 onions
  • Green chilies
  • Karahi masala

Steps

  1. Fry onions in oil.
  2. Add tomatoes, chilies & spices.
  3. Add chicken and cook until tender.

Chicken Korma

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • Fried onions
  • Korma masala

Steps

  1. Fry onions & blend with yogurt.
  2. Add chicken and masala paste.
  3. Cook until gravy thickens.
